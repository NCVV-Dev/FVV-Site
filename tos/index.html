Uploading a visual config
1.0) Visual configs should not be similar in any way, same name of the visual config or same color / settings patters might get denied, except menu colorscheme, fonts/font settings.
1.1) It's highly recommended to fill all the fields when submitting a visual config. This affects the time spent reviewing the visual config.
1.2) Submitted visual configs through the uploading page has a higher priority than configs that has been sent directly to the dev / public telegram chat.
1.3) By clicking "Submit" or "Download" button You agree with FVV that You will provide Your IP address. Your IP address is stored privately and is used only in case of suspension. IP addresses are stored for three days, after this period of time they are automatically erased from the web server.
1.4) FVV appreciates the honesty and decency of a user. Therefore, FVV do not welcome indecent names of visual configs or their description. This behavior can lead to suspending Your IP from using our services.
1.5) When your visual config is under review, your visual config description / name might be changed without your notice. This is done to avoid your own suspension from our services (Your visual config name will be "Fedoraware Visual Config" and there will be no description). By 1.4 FVV has a right to deny or suspend any user who has submitted a visual config that includes rude / swear names / description.

2.0) Any kind of attempt to bypass uploading system (Spamming, submitting the same visual config under different name, using scripts to automatically upload more than one visual config at the time, 3rd party web-bots, custom crawlers) will be considered as a threat to the website and it's services, resulting in Your or a threat's IP permanently suspended from using our services. If You cannot access the website or You think You were denied/suspended from using our services was a mistake, You can always reach us out at Telegram.

3.0) Any kind of attempt to spam download links or abuse download counter of the visual configs may result in suspension of Yours or threat's IP address.

F.A.Q

Q: I want to upload my visuals, how do I do it?
A: You can submit your own visual config at Submit Page
Q: My visuals break completely and turn into a mess after importing a visual config. Is the file corrupted?
A: It's a common bug that happens when you try to import a Fedoraware visual config. Restarting a game and then trying loading it again fixes the issue
Q: Someone just submitted my own visuals I made without me knowing, what can I do?
A: Unfortunately, FVV cannot help. Once config is uploaded on the website, it has a right to stay under uploader's name
Q: What is idenity verification? Should I fill it as well when submitting a visual config?
A: No, you should not as long as you're not the moderator of FVV. It's made for moderators only so no one could claim their username
Q: I submitted my visual config and it still didn't appear on the website. Why?
A: To submit and verify / upload your visual config it can take from 1 to 6 working days. If it did not appear even after, You can consider it was denied. Or you can always message us here
Q: What contacts I can leave when I am submitting my config?
A: As our TOS says - You can leave both of your steam and telegram accounts. We suggest you to link steam account to be sure no one will claim your telegram username after you've changed your telegram username
Q: I want to know where or at what state my visual config is. Can I know such information?
A: Yes, you can. Such question can be answered at public telegram chat
Q: I wanna help with the project development! Is there any options?
A: Of course. Your suggestions will be listened at our public telegram chat or at GitHub
Q: What does the colors and effects of the uploader's name represent?
A: This represents how much a person contributed to the website. Here's list of colors and themes which can be applied:
